🌿 About the Club

Driving environmental awareness and sustainable practices within the GIIS community since inception.

🎯

Our Mission

To foster a culture of sustainability among students at GIIS by empowering young leaders to take meaningful environmental action. We strive to create awareness, implement eco-friendly practices, and build a community that actively contributes to a greener and healthier planet.

🔭

Our Vision

To become a leading student-led sustainability initiative that inspires schools worldwide to adopt green practices. We envision a future where every student understands their environmental responsibility and acts as a steward of the Earth — creating lasting, positive impact for generations to come.

📋

Our Objectives

  • Promote biodiversity awareness through hands-on activities and the QR Plant Garden
  • Organize regular tree plantations, recycling drives, and clean-up campaigns
  • Collaborate with WWF and other organizations to amplify impact
  • Educate the school community through workshops, presentations, and events
  • Develop student leadership in environmental advocacy
  • Reduce the school's ecological footprint through actionable initiatives
💚

Impact on GIIS Community

The Sustainability Sphere Club has transformed how students at GIIS engage with the environment. Through our initiatives, we have:

  • Planted over 200 trees across school and community spaces
  • Engaged 50+ active members across multiple grade levels
  • Organized 30+ impactful events including clean-up drives and workshops
  • Established the 4th floor QR Plant Garden — an interactive learning space
  • Built a formal partnership with WWF for global conservation alignment
  • Inspired a school-wide shift towards eco-conscious behavior

Who We Are

A passionate group of students committed to making environmental stewardship a way of life.

The Sustainability Sphere Club is a student-led environmental initiative at GIIS (Global Indian International School). Founded with the belief that young people hold the power to shape a sustainable future, our club brings together students from all grade levels who share a passion for the environment.

From tree plantations and recycling drives to collaborating with WWF and maintaining our signature QR Plant Garden on the 4th floor, every initiative is planned and executed by students. Our advisors and teacher coordinators provide guidance, but the energy, creativity, and drive come from the students themselves.

Whether you're a nature enthusiast, a budding scientist, or simply someone who cares about our planet — there's a place for you in the Sustainability Sphere. Together, we are growing a greener tomorrow. 🌿
